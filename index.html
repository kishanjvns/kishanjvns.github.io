About

I'm a backend engineer who's happiest deep in the hands-on layer of a system — the part where a Kafka consumer has to be idempotent, a JWT has to validate against the right realm, and a circuit breaker has to fail in exactly the right way.

Most recently I've been building a multi-tenant healthcare platform on AWS EKS: per-tenant databases and Keycloak realms, a KrakenD gateway doing centralized auth, and an event backbone stitched together with Kafka, Debezium CDC, and the outbox pattern.

I care about correctness under concurrency, clean service boundaries drawn from domain logic, and observability that actually tells you what's wrong at 2am. I lean toward deep IC work over org charts.
